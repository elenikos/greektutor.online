Essential Greek Phrases for Tourists

Learn useful Greek phrases for everyday situations in Greece

If you are visiting Greece, learning a few Greek phrases can make your trip easier and more enjoyable.

Useful Greek Phrases

English Greek Pronunciation
Hello Γεια σου ya soo
Thank you Ευχαριστώ ef-ha-ri-STO
Please Παρακαλώ pa-ra-ka-LO
Excuse me Συγγνώμη sig-NO-mi
Do you speak English? Μιλάτε αγγλικά; mi-LA-te an-gli-KA
Where is the bathroom? Πού είναι η τουαλέτα; poo EE-ne i too-a-LE-ta
How much is this? Πόσο κάνει αυτό; PO-so KA-ni af-TO
Where is the beach? Πού είναι η παραλία; poo EE-ne i pa-ra-LI-a
Where is the hotel? Πού είναι το ξενοδοχείο; poo EE-ne to kse-no-do-HEE-o
Help Βοήθεια vo-EE-thia
