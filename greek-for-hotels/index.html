Greek Phrases for Hotels and Travel

Learn useful Greek phrases for everyday situations in Greece

If you are staying at a hotel in Greece, these Greek phrases can help you communicate with hotel staff and handle common travel situations more easily.

Useful Greek Phrases

English Greek Pronunciation
I have a reservation Έχω κράτηση E-ho KRA-ti-si
Where is my room Πού είναι το δωμάτιό μου poo EE-ne to do-MA-ti-o moo
The key please Το κλειδί παρακαλώ to kli-THEE pa-ra-ka-LO
Is breakfast included Περιλαμβάνεται πρωινό; pe-ri-lam-VA-ne-te pro-i-NO
Call a taxi Καλέστε ταξί ka-LE-ste ta-KSEE
Where is the airport Πού είναι το αεροδρόμιο; poo EE-ne to a-e-ro-DRO-mi-o
Where is the bus stop Πού είναι η στάση λεωφορείου; poo EE-ne i STA-si le-o-fo-REE-ou
What time is breakfast Τι ώρα είναι το πρωινό; ti O-ra EE-ne to pro-i-NO
I need a doctor Χρειάζομαι γιατρό hri-A-zo-me ya-TRO
Call the police Καλέστε την αστυνομία ka-LE-ste tin a-sti-no-MI-a
