Greek Phrases for Restaurants

Learn useful Greek phrases for everyday situations in Greece

When eating out in Greece, knowing a few Greek restaurant phrases can make the experience smoother and more enjoyable. Here are some useful expressions you can use when ordering food or asking for the bill.

Useful Greek Phrases

English Greek Pronunciation
The menu please Το μενού παρακαλώ to me-NOO pa-ra-ka-LO
The bill please Το λογαριασμό παρακαλώ to lo-ga-ri-a-SMO pa-ra-ka-LO
One coffee please Έναν καφέ παρακαλώ E-nan ka-FE pa-ra-ka-LO
One beer please Μία μπύρα παρακαλώ MI-a BEE-ra pa-ra-ka-LO
Water please Νερό παρακαλώ ne-RO pa-ra-ka-LO
Cheers Στην υγειά σας stin yi-YA sas
Very delicious Πολύ νόστιμο po-LEE NOS-ti-mo
More bread please Περισσότερο ψωμί παρακαλώ pe-ris-SO-te-ro pso-MEE pa-ra-ka-LO
I am vegetarian Είμαι χορτοφάγος EE-me hor-to-FA-gos
No meat please Χωρίς κρέας παρακαλώ ho-REES KRE-as pa-ra-ka-LO
