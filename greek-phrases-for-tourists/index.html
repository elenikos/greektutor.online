50 Greek Phrases Every Tourist Should Know

Essential Greek expressions for travel in Greece

If you're planning a trip to Greece, learning a few basic Greek phrases can make your experience much more enjoyable. Locals appreciate it when visitors try to speak their language, even if it's just a few words.

Below you'll find 50 useful Greek phrases for tourists, including greetings, polite expressions, and common travel questions.
